About Us

Mission Statement

Aural skills can often be the bane of a music studentâ€™s existence. Strengthening such aural abilities takes a great deal of time and practice on a daily basis. The goal of this website is to allow music students to conveniently and consistently practice specific aural exercises using quality audio even when they do not have constant access to a piano. Furthermore, other programs do not cater to the University of Puget Sound music department curriculum, leaving noticeable gaps in the exercise tools available to students. By filling in these gaps, this website hopes to present a more comprehensive education to music students. In the end, we hope the free, easy-to-navigate website, thorough exercises, and harmonious audio files will invite rather than dissuade students to hone their abilities in music theory.
